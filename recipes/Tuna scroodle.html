Tuna Scroodle

Description

This is a variation on the Tuna Skroodle found in Souper Salad. Delightful as a main dish, side dish, or snack with a tiny scoop.

Ingredients

  • 1 can Tuna (in water), drained
  • 1/2 cup Red Onion, chopped
  • 1/4 cup Green Onion, thinly chopped
  • 3 Tablespoons of Kraft Mayonnaise with Olive Oil
  • 2 tsp Black Pepper
  • 2 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 dash of Salt (optional)
  • 1/2 box (@ 3.5 oz) of Ronzoni Smart Taste Rotini

Steps

  1. Boil pasta for time to your taste (7-12 mins).
  2. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  3. While pasta is boiling, in a large bowl combine: tuna (drained), green and red onions, pepper, garlic powder, salt and mayo. Stir to blend well. Once pasta cools, add pasta and toss to coat.
